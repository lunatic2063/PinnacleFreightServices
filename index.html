About Us

Pinnacle Freight Services is a veteran-owned and operated business dedicated to redefining the standard of freight delivery. Backed by a team of industry experts, we’ve developed comprehensive solutions for businesses of all sizes, from local retailers to nationwide distributors.

Our philosophy is simple—deliver on time, communicate transparently, and ensure your freight is handled with the utmost care. With years of experience navigating complex shipping challenges, we leverage cutting-edge technology and a robust network of trusted carriers to guarantee dependable service.

Whether you need to move a single pallet or manage an entire supply chain, we tailor our approach to meet your unique requirements. Let us handle the logistics while you focus on growing your business.

Our Services

From local deliveries to large-scale logistics operations, we've got you covered:

  • Freight Shipping: We offer both full truckload and less-than-truckload (LTL) solutions, ensuring your goods arrive safely and on schedule. Our dedicated fleet and reliable carriers work around the clock to provide seamless transportation services tailored to your specific needs.
  • Logistics Consultation: Get expert advice on how to streamline your shipping process, optimize routes, and cut costs. Our consultants analyze your current logistics operations and implement strategies that enhance efficiency, reduce expenses, and improve overall supply chain performance.
  • Warehousing & Distribution: Take advantage of secure storage facilities and efficient distribution channels for your inventory. Our state-of-the-art warehouses are equipped with the latest technology to manage your stock effectively, ensuring timely distribution and minimizing downtime.
